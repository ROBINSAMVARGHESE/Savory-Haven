Our Story

Founded in 1985, Savory Haven has become a cornerstone of the Bangalore culinary scene. We take pride in serving delicious meals made from the freshest ingredients, combined with a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Notable Milestones:

  • 1985: LOLA Restaurant opens its doors for the first time.
  • 1990: Introduced our signature Butter Chicken recipe, a dish loved by all.
  • 2000: Expanded to include a full-service bar, offering a diverse selection of beverages.
  • 2010: Received multiple awards for excellence in cuisine, recognizing our commitment to quality.
  • 2020: Launched a successful online ordering platform, making our dishes accessible to everyone.
